projects

The Golden Scheme

August 2019 - August 2020

Also known as "The Golden Scheme: A Claude von Riegen Zine". This anthology contains 100+ pages of both writing and artwork celebrating the beloved Fire Emblem character. Our donations added up to $6,009 for Gamers Outreach and an additional $1,681 for the American Civil Liberties Union.

https://twitter.com/claudefanzine

Thor Tours

May 2019 - October 2019

Following the journey of Marvel's Asgard friends, the Thor Tours artbook contains 50 pages of adventure illustrated by over 40 contributors. We raised $7,322 for UNICEF's Syria Crisis Mission.

https://twitter.com/ThorToursZine

Eclipsis

January 2020 - July 2020

Full title "Eclipsis: A Dimiclaude Fanzine." This zine is dedicated to a Fire Emblem pairing between Claude von Riegen and Dimitri Alexandre Blaiddyd centering around celestial and starry themes. With this project, we donated $1,145 to the St. Vincent de Paul Society's COVID relief program in Australia.

https://twitter.com/dimiclaudezine

Bound Together

January 2023 - December 2023

Or "VashWood Zine: Bound Together," this collection of artwork and writing depicts Vash the Stampede and Nicholas D. Wolfwood from the Trigun series as partners. After working dilligently with voice actor TizziQ to deliver a breathtaking audiobook experience, we raised $13,696.69 for the California Native Plant Society and $5155.00 for the native Tongva people to help them take care of the land.

https://twitter.com/vashwoodzine
