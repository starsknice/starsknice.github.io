about me

hi! i’m knice, and it’s knice to meet you! i like illustrating and painting characters doing things. i’m on my way to getting a college degree, so while i might not have the most amount of time, daydreaming and thinking about my original stories make the hard work worth it.

if you have any questions, please feel free to reach out to me through email or social media!
