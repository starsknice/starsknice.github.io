process

some information i will need before i begin your commission:

character information

please provide references of the character you would like me to draw!! feel free to include notes such as details you would like me to pay attention to in particular, motifs or symbols you associate with them for the vibes, or a short character description so i have an idea of who they are!!

posing

what would you like me to draw the character(s) doing? stock photos or ms paint renditions would help me greatly, but if you don’t have any in mind, text description is also fine! this goes for expressions as well!

background

if you would like a specific background color or motif/set of shapes, please let me know! anything more than a general patterned background or gradient background will incur additional costs!

your paypal email

by giving me your paypal email, you have agreed to my terms and conditions (see below)!

terms & conditions

  1. all payment will be paid upfront via paypal invoices.
    • the information given to me via your paypal account will not be used for anything other than authorizing the transaction.
  2. the artwork i send will be in 300dpi. if you have a specific canvas size in mind, please let me know before i start sketching!
  3. number of corrections allowed during the commission process (ie. the number of messages allowed to be sent for change requests with unlimited requests in the body of the message):
    • after initial sketch: 5
    • after initial flat coloring: 3
    • after initial flat coloring: 3
    • any number of corrections after that incur an additional +$10 per message
  4. this is strictly for personal use only. commercial usage will have a different rate at a flat fee, so i will not ask for royalties. please talk to me if you are planning on a commercial commisison! i would be happy to work out prices with you!
    • personal use is defined as non-commercial, you can use it to print for your own enjoyment
    • commercial usage is defined as artwork used for businesses or sales, including but not limited to marketing, advertising, and display images for businesses. yes, this includes your streamer account if your goal is to monetize!
  5. using my artwork for cryptocurrency, nfts, or the like is prohibited.
